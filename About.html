My name is Matthew Tran, I am a designer based in Toronto. Problem solving has always been my passion in life, I have always tried to come with the best solutions to any task I was faced with.

A bit of background on me I went to school for Engineering and graduated July 2017. Having left school I just took up any corporate job I could get my hands on. I found myself working at CIBC as a Quality Assurance Analyst (this was a temporary co-op position, I was lucky enough to have gained this position even though I was not a student anymore). From there I explored different career options because I came to a realization that engineering was not for me and didn't fulfill my desire to create/solve problems in an innovative manner. After many months I stumbled upon UX-design and I fell in love, from there I have been moving full force into field because I knew now was the time for myself to do what I really want to do in life.



Currently seeking to join an amazing team of like minded individuals. I'd love to talk more about design over coffee. Lets chat - thematttran@gmail.com
