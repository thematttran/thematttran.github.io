NEXT36 Design Sprint - National distruption.

Product design by Matthew Tran

"Calling all makers, builders, designers, and innovators: this is your chance to disrupt the nation"

- Next36 Canada

Invitation was open to up to 100 bright, entrepreneurial minds to take over Lighthouse Labs in Toronto and build, hack, design, and prototype projects around the theme of "National Disruption". The winning team received a spot at the Next 36 National Selection Weekend in December 2017.


Onto the project itself: TAG – “Got space? we’ll take it”. International shipping is an expensive, lengthy and archaic process. Our product, it is a fun, innovative, and revolutionary method which tackles the global parcel market. A collaborative project between myself and two other individuals, for this project I was in charge of product design, crafted the UI mockups, wireframes in sketch and finished with an interactive Hi-Fi demo using invision prototyping tool.

Explore the unseen.

TAG isn't just a product to make a quick buck, we really want to promote a lifestyle of exploration, to never lose your sense of wonder. Whether you are in a different country or just in your home town you will never know whats out there unless you go and venture into the unknown.

From left to right: Jerry, Me & Mitul.

Our team placed 3rd out of 5 finalist and 100 individuals. Although we didn't win I still managed learn alot from this event, it gave me an opportunity to create/design a new product from scratch, tested my ability to work in a time crunch, apply my system level thinking to a project and further improve my team work skills. Thanks for reading!
